Towny Advanced is one of the oldest and most feature-rich Minecraft server plugins still in active development. It gives players and server administrators a complete society simulation — players form **Towns**, towns join **Nations**, and a grid-based claim system keeps everyone's land protected. With deep economy integration, granular permissions, and an extensive Java API, Towny powers thousands of multiplayer servers worldwide.

## What Towny provides

Towny organises your server's players into a three-tier hierarchy and enforces land protection at every level:

- **Residents** — every player who joins a Towny-enabled server is a Resident. They can join or found a Town, own Plots, and carry personal permission flags.
- **Towns** — a group of Residents led by a Mayor. Towns claim chunks of land (TownBlocks), set taxes, build outposts, manage jails, and choose their own flag of alliance or neutrality.
- **Nations** — coalitions of Towns led by a King. Nations set diplomacy (allies, enemies), levy taxes on member towns, and can declare neutrality.

## Economy & integrations

Towny integrates with **Vault** (and compatible economy plugins) to power town and nation banks, plot purchase prices, taxes, upkeep, and spawn costs. Optional integrations include:

- **PlaceholderAPI** — expose Towny data in scoreboards, chat, and other plugins
- **LuckPerms / GroupManager** — use Towny rank contexts in permission assignments
- **CoreProtect** — block-logging for claimed land events
- **Dynmap-Towny** — render town and nation boundaries on a live map
